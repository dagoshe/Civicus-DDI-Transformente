The Spine · Five Dimensions

From access
to agency.

Each rung is a diagnostic question you ask of your own program — layered through an intersectional lens. Inclusion that stops at access leaves people as users. Agency makes them authors.

01

Access

Who can connect — on whose device, on whose network, at what time of day?

02

Literacy

Can people use the tool — and do they trust what it asks of them?

03

Affordability

What does participation cost in data, money, and unpaid time?

04

Safety

Is the space safe to enter for women and marginalised users?

05

Agency

Who decides, shapes, and owns the digital civic process?

The Vanity Metric

One number
hides the rest.

A single connectivity statistic conceals more than it reveals. The divide is not the absence of a signal — it is time poverty, shared devices, and who gets to decide.
